This was a partner project for my final project for an artificial intelligence class at the University of Minnesota.

The purpose of this project was to predictor the sentiment of a song. There were 7 categories: happy, anger, funny, hurt, calm, romantic, and inspirational. Our program created a Naive Bayes (NB) classifier as well as a Support Vector Model (SVM) based on 400+ songs where the sentiments were known. Once these classifiers were created.
