Philippines, Winter 2017

Visiting Manila, Davao City, Iloilo City, and Hong Kong from December 28 to January 12, 2018.

During the winter break of my Junior year of college I took a trip to the Philippines and Hong Kong with my uncle, cousin and cousin's boyfriend.

We visited 6 islands in the Philippine archipelago including Luzon, Mindinau, Panay, Negros, Samal, and Corregidor. Additionally we took a flight up to Hong Kong for one day.

Manila

Markets, Culture, Chaos

Our first night was spent in Manila. We were welcomed with humidity, diesel smoke, and crowded streets. This was my first time being in Asia and it was a huge culture shock.

The next day we walked around the Baclaran Market, took the MRT in a loop around the city, and checked out Qiapo church.
